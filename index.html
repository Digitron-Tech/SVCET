🎯 Participant Guidelines & Discipline

As a premier national-level symposium, DIGITRON 26 expects all participants to maintain the highest standards of conduct, professionalism, and academic integrity.

📋 Registration Protocol

All participants must complete registration before 9:30 AM. Late entries will not be accommodated. Carry your college ID and registration confirmation at all times during the event.

🎓 Code of Conduct

Maintain professional behavior throughout the symposium. Respect fellow participants, judges, and organizers. Any form of harassment, discrimination, or misconduct will result in immediate disqualification.

⚖️ Academic Integrity

All technical submissions must be original work. Plagiarism in any form will lead to disqualification and may affect your institution's participation. Citations must be properly documented.

⏱️ Time Management

Adhere strictly to scheduled timings for all events. Participants must report 15 minutes before their event. Late arrivals forfeit their participation slot without refund.

🏟️ Venue Discipline

Maintain cleanliness in all areas. Food and beverages are permitted only in designated zones. Mobile phones must be silenced during presentations and technical events.

🤝 Team Events Protocol

Team compositions cannot be changed after registration. All team members must be present during their event. Inter-college teams are welcome but must be declared during registration.

💻 Technical Requirements

Participants must bring their own laptops for coding and development events. Organizers provide basic infrastructure but not personal equipment. Test your setup before the event begins.

🏆 Judging & Appeals

All judging decisions are final. Questions regarding results should be directed to coordinators within 30 minutes of result announcement. Maintain decorum during result declarations.

🚫 Prohibited Items

Weapons, alcohol, illegal substances, or any items that compromise safety are strictly prohibited. Violation will result in immediate removal from the premises and potential legal action.

⚠️ Important Note

Any violation of these guidelines may result in disqualification without refund of registration fees. The organizing committee reserves the right to take appropriate action in unforeseen circumstances. By participating in DIGITRON 26, you agree to abide by all rules and regulations set forth by the organizing committee.
